Capital Crescent Trail

  • Scenic views of the Potomac River
  • Connects to C&O Canal Towpath

Metropolitan Branch Trail

  • Union Station to Silver Spring (8 miles)

  • Direct route to NoMa, Brookland

Health Benefits of Cycling

Cardiovascular Health

Regular cycling strengthens your heart muscles, lowers resting pulse, and reduces blood fat levels. It can decrease the risk of cardiovascular diseases by up to 50%.

Mental Wellbeing

Cycling releases endorphins, reducing stress and anxiety. The outdoor activity can improve mood, boost creativity, and enhance overall mental health.

Weight Management

An hour of cycling can burn 400-1000 calories, depending on intensity. It's an excellent low-impact way to maintain healthy weight and boost metabolism.

Joint Health

As a low-impact exercise, cycling improves joint mobility without stressing your joints. Perfect for overall joint health and arthritis management.
